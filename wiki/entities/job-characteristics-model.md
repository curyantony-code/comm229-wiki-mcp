# Job Characteristics Model (Hackman & Oldham)

## What It Is
A motivational model of job design proposing that specific core job characteristics create psychological states that drive positive work outcomes. The model includes a moderating variable: the employee's growth need strength (desire for development challenges).

## Components

### Core Job Characteristics (5)
| Characteristic | Definition | Psychological State |
|---|---|---|
| **Skill variety** | Opportunity to use multiple skills and talents | → Meaningfulness of work |
| **Task identity** | Doing a complete piece of work from beginning to end | → Meaningfulness of work |
| **Task significance** | Impact on lives or work of others (inside or outside the org) | → Meaningfulness of work |
| **Autonomy** | Freedom to schedule own work activities and decide procedures | → Responsibility for outcomes |
| **Feedback** | Direct and clear information about effectiveness of performance | → Knowledge of results |

### Psychological States → Outcomes
- **Experienced meaningfulness** → ↑ Motivation
- **Responsibility for outcomes** → ↑ Performance
- **Knowledge of results** → ↑ Satisfaction; ↓ Absenteeism; ↓ Turnover

### Work Design Model Extension (Humphrey, Nahrang & Morgeson, 2007)
Adds beyond the JCM:
- **Social characteristics:** Interdependence, feedback from others, social support, interaction outside the org
- **Work context:** Physical demands, ergonomics, work conditions

## Common Misconceptions
- JCM is about job **design** (changing the structure), not just individual differences
- Autonomy affects *responsibility* specifically — not meaningfulness (that's variety/identity/significance)
- Feedback in JCM is from the **job itself** (not from supervisors) — distinct from managerial feedback

## Job Design Strategies
- **Job rotation:** Move employees between jobs (↑ variety)
- **Job enlargement:** Add tasks (horizontal — ↑ variety)
- **Job enrichment:** Add higher-order responsibilities (vertical — ↑ autonomy, significance)
- **Employee involvement:** Employee customizes own work
- **Work teams:** Work organized collectively
